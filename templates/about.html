About My Site

This site contains a simple application that can identify places of interest within walking distance of any location you input into the Application. It can also aid in budgeting for any trip giving a recommended food allowance and showing how much money you have remaining for attractions and souvenirs This site uses postgresql to store user data and only users can access the application, It enables the use of the flask extentions wtforms and sqlalchemy.
